Reasons

#Spreadsheet ColumnDetailsRequired?
1Access ReasonsThe available access reasons that can be selected on NAR creation. These are also used for customer notification reasonsYes
2Decline NAR ReasonsThe available reasons that can be selected for declining a NAR submission.Yes
3More Info Required ReasonsThe available reasons that can be selected for requesting more information on a NAR submission.Yes
4Within Lead Time ReasonsThe available reasons for submitting a NAR within the minimum lead time required for the job type.Yes
5Within Lead Time CausesThe available causes for why a NAR is submitted within the minimum lead time required for the job type.Yes
6Cancel NAR ReasonsThe available reasons for why a submitted NAR is cancelled. These are also used for customer notification cancellation reasons.Yes
7Recall NAR ReasonsThe available reasons for why recalling a NAR.Yes
note

Within EDNAR, a comment box is available to provide more information with any of these selections.
